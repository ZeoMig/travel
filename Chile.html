Easter Island
On Easter Day in 1722, Dutch captain Jacob Roggeveen stumbled upon an island in the central Pacific Ocean. He became the first European to set foot on this secluded piece of land. in the ship's log, Roggeveen marked it as "Easter Island". Easter Island, or Rapa Nui, is an island in the Pacific Ocean in Chile, famous for its giant stone statues.
